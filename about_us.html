About Us

At LohaKart, we are transforming the way businesses source, procure, and manage raw materials. As a leading B2B e-commerce digital platform, we provide manufacturers, suppliers, and procurement professionals with a streamlined, efficient, and data-driven solution for sourcing raw materials across industries. Our platform connects businesses with a wide range of high quality materials with multiple grades and sizes with delivery at different sites just a click away so companies need not to worry and can focus on what they do best: growing their business.

What We Do

At LohaKart, we specialize in providing comprehensive solutions for the metal supply chain, aimed at enhancing efficiency and quality for our clients. Our services encompass sourcing high-quality raw materials, offering precision fabrication, and managing reliable logistics to ensure timely delivery. We leverage advanced technology and industry expertise to streamline operations, allowing businesses to focus on their core activities.

Innovative Solutions

At LohaKart, we pride ourselves on delivering innovative solutions that address the evolving needs of the metal supply chain.
